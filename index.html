About

Built around the vehicle, not the checklist.

The Detailing Studio started with a simple frustration: most "details" are a rushed wash and a spray of tire shine. We wanted appointments that actually respected the car — proper paint decontamination, correction measured under real light, and coatings applied the way the manufacturer intends.

Every booking, mobile or in the studio bay, follows the same process regardless of the vehicle in front of us — a daily driver gets the same attention as a weekend car.
